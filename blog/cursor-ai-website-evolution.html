Evolving My Website with Cursor AI

Posted on May 7, 2025 in Technology

The Journey Begins

As someone who loves solving problems and driving efficiency, I'm always looking for ways to improve my workflow. Recently, I discovered Cursor AI, an intelligent code editor that promised to revolutionize how I work with code. What better way to test it than by enhancing my personal website with a blog feature?

Planning the Evolution

My website started as a simple portfolio site showcasing my projects and experiences. While it served its purpose, I wanted to add a space where I could share my thoughts, experiences, and insights more dynamically. Cursor AI helped me plan and implement this evolution step by step.

The Implementation Process

With Cursor AI's assistance, we:

  • Created a dedicated blog section with a clean, consistent design
  • Implemented a search functionality to help readers find relevant content
  • Set up a category system for better content organization
  • Developed a template system for future blog posts

The Power of AI-Assisted Development

What impressed me most about using Cursor AI was how it understood my requirements and provided context-aware suggestions. It helped me:

  • Maintain consistent styling across new pages
  • Implement responsive design elements
  • Set up proper navigation and linking
  • Create a search system that works seamlessly

Looking Forward

This is just the beginning of my website's evolution. With the blog infrastructure in place, I'm excited to share more about my experiences, insights, and learnings. The combination of Cursor AI's capabilities and my vision has created a platform that's not just a portfolio, but a space for continuous learning and sharing.

Stay tuned for more posts about technology, personal experiences, and professional insights. The journey of evolution continues!
